Quick & Healthy Breakfast Bowl Recipe

April 7, 2024 by Jonathan

Start your day with this nutritious and delicious breakfast bowl! Here's what you need:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1 sliced banana
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

Simply layer the ingredients in a bowl, starting with yogurt as the base. Top with fruits, sprinkle with granola and chia seeds, and drizzle with honey. Ready in 5 minutes!
